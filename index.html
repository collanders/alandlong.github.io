When I think of design, I'm reminded of the haunting end to Fitzgerald's The Great Gatsby:



So we beat on, boats against the current, borne back ceaselessly into the past.


Where we are going as designers and developers is shaped entirely on where we've been. Design & development has seen massive changes over the last 20 years, and yet in the last few, a widespread return to the simplistic and minimal has occurred.



I find a lot of beauty in the design patterns we have seen emerge recently. It makes Fitzgerald feel even more prophetic than ever to me - while we toil to push the boundaries of our work, we shouldn't lose sight of the things we've learned & passed along the way.
